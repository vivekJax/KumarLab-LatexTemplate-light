\section{Introduction}

Protein folding is one of the most challenging problems in computational biology. The ability to predict protein structures from amino acid sequences has profound implications for drug discovery, disease understanding, and biotechnology applications.

Recent advances in deep learning have revolutionized protein structure prediction, with methods like AlphaFold \cite{jumper2021highly} achieving remarkable accuracy. Similarly, RoseTTAFold \cite{baek2021accurate} has demonstrated the power of three-track neural networks in this domain.
